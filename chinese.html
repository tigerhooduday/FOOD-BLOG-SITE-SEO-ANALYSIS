Featured Chinese Dishes

Kung Pao Chicken

A spicy stir-fry dish made with chicken, peanuts, and vegetables.

Ingredients: Chicken, peanuts, vegetables, soy sauce, chili peppers.

Preparation: Marinate chicken, stir-fry with vegetables and sauce, add peanuts.

Cultural Significance: Named after a Qing Dynasty official, popular Sichuan dish.
