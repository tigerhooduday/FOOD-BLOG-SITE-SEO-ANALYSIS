Featured Korean Dishes

Bibimbap

A mixed rice dish topped with various vegetables, meat, and a fried egg.

Ingredients: Rice, vegetables (such as carrots, spinach, and mushrooms), beef, egg, gochujang (red chili paste).

Preparation: Cook rice, prepare toppings, assemble in a bowl, mix with gochujang sauce.

Cultural Significance: Traditional Korean dish, known for its colorful presentation and balance of flavors.
