Featured Indian Dishes

Butter Chicken

A creamy and mildly spiced chicken dish cooked in a tomato-based sauce.

Ingredients: Chicken, tomatoes, cream, butter, spices.

Preparation: Marinate chicken, cook in sauce, finish with cream and butter.

Cultural Significance: Originated in Delhi, popular worldwide.

Palak Paneer

Palak Paneer is a creamy Indian dish made with spinach (palak) and paneer (Indian cottage cheese).

Ingredients: Spinach, paneer, tomatoes, onions, garlic, ginger, spices.

Preparation: Blanch spinach, saut√© with spices, blend into a puree, cook with paneer cubes.

Cultural Significance: Popular North Indian dish, often served with rice or naan.

Chole Bhature

Chole Bhature is a famous North Indian dish consisting of spicy chickpea curry (chole) served with deep-fried bread (bhature).

Ingredients: Chickpeas, onions, tomatoes, ginger, garlic, spices, flour, yogurt.

Preparation: Soak and cook chickpeas, prepare curry with spices, ferment dough for bhature, deep-fry until golden brown.

Cultural Significance: Popular street food in India, often served with pickles and onions.
